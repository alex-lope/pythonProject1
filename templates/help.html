Help with Graduation Success Model Interface

Inputting Text

Text may be input into the field by typing or copy-and-pasting text from another source. Text is limited to 500 words.

Interpreting the Results

  • Word Count: The number of words in the submission are counted and the count presented.
  • Sentiment Score: Sentiment measures the tone of the writing, from negative tone (negative values to -1) to positive tone (positive values up to 1).
  • Readability Score: Based on the Flesch-Kincaid Readability Scale, the readability score presents the sampleâ€™s writing grade level.
  • SVM Prediction: Presents the classification of successful or unsuccessful and the probability of success based on the model.
  • RNN Prediction: Presents the classification of successful or unsuccessful and the probability of success based on the model.
  • CNN Prediction: Presents the classification of successful or unsuccessful and the probability of success based on the model.
  • Ensemble Prediction: Presents the classification of successful or unsuccessful. The ensemble method is the determining prediction for the writing sample.
